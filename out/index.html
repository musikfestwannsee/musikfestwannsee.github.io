Artists

Piano

Cello

Locations

Discover the beautiful venues around Wannsee where our concerts will take place

Main Stage at Liebermann Villa

The historic Liebermann Villa provides an elegant setting for our main concerts, with its beautiful architecture and gardens overlooking the Wannsee.

  • •Seating capacity: 300
  • •Steinway D concert grand piano
  • •Accessible facilities

Lakeside Pavilion

An intimate open-air venue perfect for chamber music, offering stunning views across the water.

Wannsee Terrace

A charming outdoor space for afternoon recitals, surrounded by lush gardens and lake vistas.

House of the Wannsee Conference

A historically significant venue with excellent acoustics for smaller ensemble performances.
